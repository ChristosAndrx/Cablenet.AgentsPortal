Plume Setup
In order to configure your Plume Adaptive WiFiTM network, you will need the following:
  • Internet Service
  • Access to email
  • Modem
  • Data or Wi-Fi Connection on your mobile device to download the Plume App and set up your Plume network.
  • Mobile Device
  • BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) support, (Bluetooth 4.0 or higher)
  • Android Requirements: Phone device running Android 4.4 or later

You will need to download the Plume Android Application to configure your Pods.

The latest version of the Plume Application for Android: Plume for Android
The latest version of the Plume Application for iOS: Plume for iOS


Plume Network Mode
  • Auto Mode: The Default configuration. Plume will auto detect the configuration and set itself up accordingly.
  • Router Mode: Plume Gateway acts as secure firewall, handles DCHP, DNS, and all other network security.
  • Bridge Mode: Plume Gateway is connected to a wired router or switch. Plume will handle broadcasting your WiFi and leave all other routing functions to your router.

Setting up your plume
  1. Account setup: Full Name & Email Address should be registered in the Plume Application
  2. Account password: Must be at least 6 characters long.
  3. Email verification: Email Address must be verified.
  4. Restart your modem: Unplug any devices currently connected on the modem before restarting.
  5. Connect on modem: Connect the pod on the modem via ethernet cable & hit next in the application.
  6. Connect to power: Plug the second pod on any power socket. Once the pod is detected by the application and associated to the cloud, the screen will automatically advance.
  7. Set up your new WiFi: Enter your new SSID & Password Note: Not to be confused with the password used earlier.
  8. Pod updates: Pods will check for firmware updates during setup & periodically over time.
  9. Join your network: On Android, the application will automatically join the new network.
  10. Naming Pods: Pods can be named for easier identification.
    The phone needs to be in close proximity & the bluetooth needs to be enabled, in order to proceed with changes.

Network Optimization:

The plume network will optimize performance over:

  • The next 15-30 minutes for the first attempt.
  • The mext 24 hours for a more detailed optimization.
  • The mext few weeks for fine tuning.

⚠ Speedtest should always take place directly from the modem with an ethernet cable.
✘ Checking the speed test screen in the plume application is not recommended.
