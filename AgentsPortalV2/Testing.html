Front Panel Indicators (Left to Right):

  1. Tel 2: Indicates the status of telephone line 2.
  2. Tel 1: Indicates the status of telephone line 1.
  3. Secure: Indicates whether the Wireless Protected Setup (WPS) is active.
  4. WiFi: Indicates the status of the wireless connectivity.
  5. Ethernet: Indicates ethernet connectivity between the Modem and the connected devices.
  6. Online: Indicates Internet data transmission status.
  7. US: Indicates upstream connectivity.
  8. DS: Indicates downstream connectivity.
  9. Power: Indicates whether AC power is available to the unit.
  10. Power Switch: Power On/Off switch
LED Status:
  1. Ethernet LED:
    • On: Device Connected.
    • Off: No Device Connected.
    • Flashing: Device Activity.
  2. WiFi LED:
    • On: Device Connected.
    • Off: No Device Connected.
    • Flashing: Device Activity.
  3. Tel 1 & 2 LED:
    • On: ON Hook
    • Off: Disabled
    • Blinking: Off Hook
  4. DS LED:
    • Yellow: Connected to the internet (High Speed).
    • Green: Connected to the internet (Ultra High Speed).
    • Blinking: Not connected to the internet.
  5. US LED:
    • Yellow: Connected to the internet (High Speed).
    • Green: Connected to the internet (Ultra High Speed).
    • Blinking: Not connected to the internet.


Rear Panel Indicators (Left to Right):

  1. WPS Button:
  2. Reset Button: Factory resets the device if pressed with a non-metalic object.
  3. USB Port:Disabled.
  4. telephone 1 (Grey Port): Connector for the first phone line.
  5. telephone 2 (Grey Port): Connector for the second phone line.
  6. Ethernet 1-4 (Yellow Ports): Connectors for ethernet cables.
  7. Coax Cable: Connector for the coax cable.
  8. Power: Connector for the power cable.
