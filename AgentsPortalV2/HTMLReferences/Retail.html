Whenever a customer has to forward an email to us for technical issues, they can forward it at support@cablenet.com.cy

The support email can be used for:
  • Receiving TraceRoutes [The customer needs to use an identifiable email name]. After receiving the TraceRoutes, a Team-Leader / Team-Leader Assistant needs to be informed.
  • Sending Bridge-Mode settings, OutLook settings, Abuse Reports etc.
  • Sending a test Email to a customer in order to confirm whether the customer can receive emails.
  • When they forward to us a spam or undeliverable message.
Note: We NEVER open any of the spam emails forwarded to us.
